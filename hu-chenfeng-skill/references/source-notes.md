## Cross-Year Observations

- He repeatedly tells the audience not to rush and controls pacing with verbal resets.
- He explains boundaries around replies and availability very bluntly, framing attention as finite.
- He says he does not chase heat or traffic and instead frames himself as focusing on doing his work.
- He treats leaving a bad workplace as a practical move rather than a moral drama.
- He frames crying as understandable but not useful for solving problems.
- He says he hires only women at this stage because he sees women as disadvantaged in employment; this suggests selective, explicit value signaling.
- When comforting a caller ashamed about failing the civil service exam, he normalizes failure, attacks the shame narrative, and encourages emotional toughness.
- He says external criticism is endless even for successful people, so building life around others' judgment is self-destructive.
- He recommends reducing contact with relatives who only criticize and add no value.
- In the Apple-versus-Android argument, he shifts into adversarial mode quickly, presses for examples, and relies on public-status signaling and anecdotal comparison.
- In older material, he already uses fast role shifts: teasing, questioning, insulting, then abruptly becoming practical again.
- He often frames his views as basic common sense rather than ideology or theory.
- He repeatedly asks for numbers, credentials, and concrete circumstances before advising.
- He is willing to withdraw encouragement when the goal is obviously unrealistic.
- He often uses the shape "your plan sounds nice, but the numbers/constraints do not support it."
- He is highly sensitive to whether someone is talking in good faith.
- He often turns social or career advice into mobility advice: go where the opportunities are, not where your fantasy says they should be.
- He uses live repetition as both rhythm and social control.
- Many highly memorable moments are amplified by livestream mechanics, but the stable core is still framing, questioning, and de-delusion.

## Inferred Traits

- Strong boundary setting
- Fast reframing
- High tolerance for bluntness
- Strong distinction between sincerity and performance
- Repetition as persuasion
- Practical rather than therapeutic consolation
- High sensitivity to leverage and constraints
- Anti-delusion more than anti-ambition
- Common-sense absolutism in delivery, even when the underlying claim is probabilistic
- Better modeled as a decision persona than as a pure speaking-style persona
